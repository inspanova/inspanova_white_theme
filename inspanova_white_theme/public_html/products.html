Edunova

Edunova is our school management system. Education is a lifelong process. It start with schools. Technology is a part of our day to day life. We live in technology. Edunova helps school management and parents to track the progress of their students.

Modules of Edunova 1.0

Attendance Management

This module helps to track the attendance of students, teachers and other staffs.

Mark management

This module helps in creating the exam calendar and record the students mark in each exams and analyze their progress.
